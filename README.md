# ventasorg-privacy
VentasOrg is a lightweight business tool designed for fast, offline-friendly sales tracking and inventory management.
VentasOrg is a streamlined business app built for entrepreneurs, small shops, and mobile vendors. Track sales, manage inventory, and stay organized — all without needing constant internet access.

Designed by a founder who understands the hustle, VentasOrg is fast, intuitive, and built for real-world use. Whether you're selling in Tijuana, shipping across borders, or testing with family, VentasOrg adapts to your workflow.

Features:
• Offline-friendly sales tracking
• Simple inventory management
• Clean, responsive interface
• Built-in privacy and transparency

This is the first public test release. Feedback is welcome — help shape the future of VentasOrg.
